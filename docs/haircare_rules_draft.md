# Haircare Interaction Rules Draft

This document contains proposed haircare compatibility rules for future implementation in the analyzer and risk engine.

---

## Rule 1: Protein Overload

**Ingredients:** Keratin + Hydrolyzed Proteins + Protein Treatments

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Using multiple protein-heavy products in the same routine can make hair stiff, brittle, and prone to breakage, especially when moisture balance is not maintained.

---

## Rule 2: Sulfates on Color-Treated Hair

**Ingredients:** Sulfates + Color-Treated Hair

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Sulfates can accelerate color fading by stripping protective oils and washing out artificial pigments.

---

## Rule 3: Silicones on Curly Hair Without Clarifying

**Ingredients:** Dimethicone + Other Silicones + Curly Hair

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Heavy silicone buildup may weigh down curls and reduce moisture penetration if clarifying products are not used regularly.

---

## Rule 4: Clarifying Shampoo After Heavy Silicone Use

**Ingredients:** Silicones + Clarifying Shampoo

**Risk Level:** Safe

**Explanation:** Clarifying products help remove accumulated silicone buildup and improve product absorption.

---

## Rule 5: High Protein + Low Moisture Routine

**Ingredients:** Protein Treatments + Lack of Moisturizing Ingredients

**Risk Level:** High

**Explanation:** Excess protein without adequate moisture may lead to increased brittleness and hair breakage.

---

## Rule 6: Excessive Clarifying Shampoo Use

**Ingredients:** Clarifying Shampoo + Frequent Use

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Overuse may strip natural oils and contribute to dryness and scalp irritation.

---

## Rule 7: Sulfates + Dry Hair

**Ingredients:** Sulfates + Dry Hair Profile

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Sulfates can worsen existing dryness and increase frizz.

---

## Rule 8: Heat Styling + Protein Treatments

**Ingredients:** Protein Treatments + Frequent Heat Exposure

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Hair may become more rigid and susceptible to damage when exposed to repeated heat styling.

---

## Rule 9: Coconut Oil Before Protein Treatment

**Ingredients:** Coconut Oil + Protein Treatments

**Risk Level:** Low

**Explanation:** Coconut oil may reduce protein loss and provide some protection during protein-based treatments.

---

## Rule 10: Multiple Leave-In Products

**Ingredients:** Multiple Leave-In Conditioners

**Risk Level:** Low

**Explanation:** Layering several leave-in products may increase buildup and reduce hair volume.

---

## Rule 11: Heavy Oils on Fine Hair

**Ingredients:** Argan Oil + Coconut Oil + Fine Hair

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Excessive oil application can weigh down fine hair and reduce volume.

---

## Rule 12: Dry Shampoo Overuse

**Ingredients:** Dry Shampoo + Frequent Application

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Excessive use can contribute to scalp buildup and irritation.

---

## Rule 13: Protein Mask + Protein Conditioner

**Ingredients:** Protein Mask + Protein Conditioner

**Risk Level:** Medium

**Explanation:** Using multiple protein-rich products simultaneously may contribute to protein overload.

---

## Rule 14: Deep Conditioner After Clarifying

**Ingredients:** Clarifying Shampoo + Deep Conditioner

**Risk Level:** Safe

**Explanation:** Deep conditioning after clarifying helps restore moisture balance.

---

## Rule 15: Panthenol + Moisturizing Routine

**Ingredients:** Panthenol + Moisturizing Ingredients

**Risk Level:** Safe

**Explanation:** Panthenol supports moisture retention and generally works well with hydrating haircare routines.
